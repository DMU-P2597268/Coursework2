Stray Kids

Stray Kids debuted on the 25th of March 2018, under JYP Entertainment. Originally a 9 piece group, Woojin left on the 27th of October 2019. They were created through the survival show of the same name and now currently consist of Leader; Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin and the youngest; I.N. Their fandom name is STAY. They debuted with the song District 9, however, they did have a pre-debut album with it's main track Hellevator. They have since released 9 albums and mini-albums. They have also done two world tours since debut and several Asian tours. You can find out more information about them on Wikipedia: Here.

The Boyz

The Boyz debuted on the 6th of December 2017 under Cre.ker Entertainment. They were originally a 12 member group but member, Hwall, left due health issues in October 2019. The group debuted with the ep, The First with their debuting song being Boy. They have since had 7 albums and mini-albums in total. The group currently consists of: Sangyeon, Jacob, Younghoon, Hyunjae, Juyeon, Kevin, New, Q, Ju Haknyeon, Sunwoo, and Eric. You can find out more information about them on Wikipedia: Here.

NCT

NCT debuted under SM Entertainment and have 23 members in total, all parcipating in separate sub-units. They debuted their first sub-group, NCT U, on the 9th April 2019. The second unit, NCT 127, followed with their debut on the 7th of July 2016. And then followed by the debut of NCT Dream on the 24th of August 2016. For several years, the groups stayed as a 15 member group until 2018 when three new members were added. All 18 members participated in a comeback that year and they continued as an 18 member group until a further three members were added in 2019 and the fourth but not final unit was debuted. WayV, the all Chinese member group debuted on the 17th January 2019. Then most recently, two new members were introduced in 2020, and participated in the whole group comeback but are also due to debut in a future sub-unit. The members include: Taeyong, Taeil, Doyoung, Jaehyun, Ten, Mark, Yuta, Winwin, Haechan, Renjun, Jeno, Jaemin, Chenle, Jisung, Johnny, Jungwoo, Lucas, Kun, Xiaojun, Hendery, Yangyang, Shotaro, and Sungchan.
